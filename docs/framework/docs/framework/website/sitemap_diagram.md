## Information Architecture Rules

1. Keep top level public pages limited and intentional.
2. Use Resources and Company groups to reduce nav clutter.
3. Avoid route naming drift.
4. Keep pricing and product easy to access.
5. Keep legal pages easy to find in the footer.

## Final Principle

The sitemap should support clarity, not just SEO sprawl. If a page does not serve acquisition, education, proof, trust, or conversion, it probably should not be top level.
